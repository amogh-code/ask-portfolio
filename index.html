My Philosophy

As someone fascinated by psychology and drawn to existentialist thought, I believe understanding human behaviour is the foundation for building things that actually matter.

Psychological Lens

My psychology background gives me a lens most builders do not have. I design for humans first, understanding behaviour before building products.

Stoic Foundation

Drawn to stoicism and existentialism. Focus on what you can control, build in the present, take responsibility. This keeps me grounded across multiple projects.
